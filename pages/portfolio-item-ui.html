During my study at CMM, one of my first assignments were to create a so called concept book for a website which was based on our own idea. The concept book was required to contain many part; one of the most important was to create a wireframe and a visual prototype. My choice was an educational website about bird watching for children.

Highlights with CSS cards

The wireframes and the visual prototypes were used for a later HTML∕CSS assignment to create the website Vogels in de achtertuin. This website is yet static as we had not yet learned how to make a responsive website at that point. Besides I can build up a responsive website with HTML∕CSS and basic Javascript from scratch.

Finally, it has to be pointed out that the target audience of this website is children at the age from 7 years. Every website needs a thorough research and that is what I do not have yet. However, Zwolle, where I live, is surrounded by nature reserves where there is an astonishingly rich diversity of bird species. It takes no more than twenty minutes by cycling to get to an amazingly beautiful nature reserves. So it is the perfect opportunity to make local children aware of the surrounding nature reserves and how important they are.
