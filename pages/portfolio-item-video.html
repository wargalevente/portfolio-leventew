Meerkoeten

My first ever video was made about a coot family. The reason is quite simple: they are one of the most numerous bird species in the Netherlands and I recorded the video in May when the chicks were already quite big. I also found a cosy music for the record but I let the bird sounds in as well. The whole coot family recording looks idillic but there is a very short cut which shows that coots can be quite nasty even to each other.

Lepelaars

The second video is about Spoonbills. In this case there were two reasons at least why I picked Spoonbills: firstly I had enough materials about spoonbills to put together, which is not always easy given that all birds are not always active and not always close enough to record them; secondly, Spoonbills are quite unique, and I had never seen them in such numbers before.

Bever

The third video is about a beaver. It was one of those days when you do your normal things and then, quite unexpectedly, something quite extraordinary happens. A beaver walked across the way in front of me and it was quite obvious that the guy was not afraid of or bothered by anything let alone me. I managed to make quite good quality two recordings that I used to create this video sequence.
